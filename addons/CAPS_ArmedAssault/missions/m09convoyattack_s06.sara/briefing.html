Don't forget that you're the pilot of this chopper, not the gunner. You can assign targets and give the order to fire - but (unless you choose "manual-fire" mode) you'll be leaving the actual shooting to your gunner. Once you've nominated a target, it's your job to orientate the chopper to point your weapons at the enemy. Your gunner will judge when they're in range, and open fire.
