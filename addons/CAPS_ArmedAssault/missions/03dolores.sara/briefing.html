A large-scale ambush is being prepared for the SLA armored force moving South from Ortego. The bridges in the city of Dolores connect all major roads in the South of the island. Taking them out is an obvious priority, but we plan on taking out a large amount of SLA armor along with them.

The city's center is located between two arms of the sea reaching inland, resulting in a peninsula-like landmass. Taking out the city's three bridges will leave any units trapped in the center with only one escape: North. At the base of the peninsula, 3rd Platoon has prepared a defensive line that will engage the enemy forces attempting to get out of the city. The remnants of 2nd Platoon will try to hold off any enemy reinforcements coming in from the East.

To further 'motivate' the enemy to move out of the center, we have placed hidden caches of M136 Anti-Tank rounds around the town for you to use.

Place satchel charges under the first bridge and find a nearby place to hide.

Upon hearing "Flash! Flash! Flash!", detonate the charges. Proceed to harrass the enemy with AT fire.

Make your way back to 3rd Platoon and help fend off the enemy troops.

Board a vehicle and proceed to rendezvous point Diamond, or retreat to the hills to the North.
